Summary: Mobility Trends vs. COVID-19 Rates

Does social distancing really matter?

The purpose of this project was to analyze how Mobility trends as a measure of social distancing affect COVID-19 trends. To accomplish this analysis, we first pulled data from the New York times for COVID-19 infection rates for each county in the United States. We also extracted the mobility rates for each county form Apple website. Mobility rates is a measure of how many driving requests are submitted on a daily basis in comparison to a pre-covid day (January 6th) as a base line (by We analyzed the dataset to identify the top 4 COVID infection counties in the United States. This was our sample size to examine the trends. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as wells as explanations and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed
